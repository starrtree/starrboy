Conditioning Systems

Engineered conditioning solutions integrated for reliability and performance.

Our environmental rooms are engineered for precision applications where temperature, humidity, and air quality must remain within exact tolerances. From pharmaceutical stability chambers to industrial process rooms, each system is designed around your specific requirements.

  • Temperature precision to +/- 0.5°C or better
  • Humidity control from 10% to 95% RH
  • Walk-in and reach-in configurations
  • Full validation and commissioning support

Our control systems integrate seamlessly with environmental and conditioning equipment, providing operators with clear visibility and precise command over every parameter. Built for reliability and serviceability, our controls are designed to evolve with your needs.

  • PLC-based and DDC control architectures
  • Remote monitoring and alarm capabilities
  • Historical data logging and reporting
  • Integration with building management systems

Our conditioning systems deliver the heating, cooling, and humidity management that environmental rooms require. Engineered as integrated solutions rather than off-the-shelf components, these systems are sized and configured for your specific application.

  • Chilled water, DX, and glycol cooling options
  • Electric, hot water, and steam heating
  • Steam generation and ultrasonic humidification
  • Service-friendly design with accessible components
